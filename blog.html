BookLoom 1.0 Released

2025 · Paperlight Suite

The first Paperlight app is out in the world. BookLoom is a small, terminal-based reading tracker that keeps everything in local JSON files.

It includes shelves for Want-to-read, Reading, Read, and DNF, along with ratings, tags, progress tracking, and a theme system.

Planning the rest of the Paperlight Suite

2025 · Notes

The long-term plan includes StoryLoom (writing), DayLoom (journaling), HabitLoom (habits), VaultLoom (private vault), and BudgetLoom (budgeting).

Like BookLoom, these apps will be offline-first and file-based, designed for people who like calm, personal tools.
