About QuietLoom

QuietLoom is a small, personal software label focused on making tools that feel calm, local, and trustworthy. The idea is simple: software that respects your attention and your privacy.

The Paperlight Suite is built for people who like books, notebooks, and slow, thoughtful work. No feeds, no infinite scroll — just small programs you can keep open while you read, write, or plan.

Principles

  • Offline first. Everything runs locally and does not require an account.
  • Human-readable data. Your data lives in simple files (like JSON) you can back up yourself.
  • Quiet by design. No pop-ups, no notifications, no pressure.
  • Small & focused. Each app does a few things well, instead of everything loudly.
