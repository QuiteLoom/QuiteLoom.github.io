Apps in the Paperlight Suite

The Paperlight Suite is a growing collection of small, focused tools created under the QuietLoom name. All apps are designed to be offline-first, file-based, and calm.

BookLoom

A terminal-based reading tracker with multiple shelves (Want-to-read, Reading, Read, DNF), ratings, tags, series tracking, and a built-in theme system.

Status: Available · Version 1.0

View details

StoryLoom (planned)

A writing organizer to keep track of story ideas, scenes, characters, and timelines — built with the same quiet philosophy as BookLoom.

Status: Early concept

DayLoom (planned)

A gentle journaling & mood tracking tool designed to feel like a small, private notebook.

HabitLoom (planned)

A simple, offline habit tracker for daily routines, streaks, and tiny wins.

VaultLoom (planned)

A privacy-focused vault for small secrets, codes, or notes — encrypted locally.

BudgetLoom (planned)

A lightweight budget & savings tracker for people who prefer simple numbers over dashboards.
